HANDS ON FROM THE GROUND UP

Experience. It’s the one quality that, more than any other, distinguishes a good builder from a truly great one. And it’s a mark of distinction John Z. Welton, President of Sunvale Homes, knows first-hand, having spent more than 25 years building a reputation for new home excellence. Starting work in his family’s company, United Lands Corporation, John learned the business of building from the ground up, eventually overseeing the development and construction of more than 2000 new townhomes, condominiums and custom crafted homes all across the Greater Toronto Area. Today John’s talents, insights and strong belief in a hands – on approach are the foundation of Sunvale Homes and their unique approach to building better homes. Sunvale’s developments in Orangeville, Mono, Shelburne and Collingwood reflect these high principles. Here you’ll find homes of extraordinary craftsmanship and attention to detail, each perfected with great luxury features that Sunvale Homes does not consider as upgrades, but come standard with every marvelous home. The common denominator in every Sunvale Homes community is quality – quality that is reflected in every design detail…quality that families notice and appreciate…and quality that translates into real comfort and enduring value that stands the test of time. These days home buyers have high expectations – meeting and exceeding those expectations is Sunvale’s ultimate goal. And you’ll experience our commitment every step of the way…from helpful salespeople, to homes of the finest quality and first rate after sales service, the Sunvale team of professionals takes great pride in making you feel at home right from the very start. Courtesy, Value and Customer Satisfaction, these are the cornerstones of the Sunvale Homes philosophy and they ring true in everything Sunvale does. A personal approach that means, no matter what your concern, you’ll always have the confidence of knowing John is there for you, just a phone call away.
